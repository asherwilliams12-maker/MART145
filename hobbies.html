Basketball

By: Asher Williams

I enjoy a variety of hobbies in my free time. My favorite is basketball

About Me

  • Basketball is a sport that I have played my whole life. I started playing when I was about 5 years old. I played on a team in my hometown of Bonners Ferry, Idaho.

  • I played all the way through high school and now I play for Montana Tech. I love the competitiveness of the game and the teamwork that is involved. It is a great way to stay in shape and have fun at the same time.

  • In high school, I was fortunate enough to break the all-time scoring record for my school. It was a great accomplishment for me and something that I am very proud of.

  • Playing basketball has taught me a lot about discipline, hard work, and perseverance. I have learned to set goals for myself and work towards achieving them. It has also helped me to develop strong friendships with my teammates and coaches.
